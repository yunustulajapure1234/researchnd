Privacy Policy

At Fisenrnd, we are dedicated to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you share with us through forms or communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, respond to your inquiries, and communicate with you. We may also use your information for marketing purposes, but you will always have the option to opt out of such communications.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as necessary to provide our services, comply with the law, or protect our rights.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us info@fisenrnd.com.
